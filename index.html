Frequently Asked Questions

"When can I post GIFs on the Discord server?"

To maintain a safe environment, only users ranked Warrant Officer or higher are allowed to post GIFs, helping us prevent inappropriate content.

"When can I post images on the Discord server?"

Posting images is reserved for users ranked above Captain to ensure an extra level of control over what is shared on the server.

"How can I join the staff team?"

Stay active on Discord, attend events, help others, and recruit new members—these are the essentials to becoming a part of our staff team! Show your dedication, and you might be next in line!

"How can I partner with Ice Warriors?"

Great news! We’re open to new partnerships. To be eligible, your server must have at least 150 members, be friendly, and comply with all Discord TOS. Ready to collaborate? Let’s team up!
